Why teams upgrade

The problems DRK was built to kill — and why Pro pays for itself.

No safety net for Docker data

Docker Desktop has shipped no backup since Docker retired Volumes Backup & Share in 2024. One docker volume rm, a disk failure, or a reinstall and your data is gone. DRK snapshots it automatically, on a schedule.

Backup scripts you don’t trust

Hand-rolled tar + cron scripts rot, go undocumented, and nobody tests the restore until it’s an outage. DRK restore-tests every backup in a sandbox, so recovery actually works.

The Free-tier ceiling

Free caps you at 5 policies and 14-day history. Pro unlocks unlimited policies and 90- or 365-day audit retention — real coverage across every stack you run.

Silent failures

A backup that quietly fails is the worst kind. Pro adds notifications (Slack, email, webhook) so you hear about a problem before you need the backup.

Your keys, your control

Pro adds bring-your-own / customer-managed encryption keys on top of the AES-256 encryption at rest that every plan already includes.

Keep it alive ♥

DRK is the only published Docker Desktop backup extension. A one-time $29 (or $99/seat) directly funds its continued development — upgrading is the simplest way to support the project and get more in return.

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before you buy.

What does “perpetual within major version” mean for Personal Pro?

Your $29 Personal Pro purchase is a one-time payment. It unlocks Pro features permanently for the major version you bought it under (for example, all v1.x releases). A future major version may be a separate upgrade.

How does Commercial Pro per-seat pricing work?

Commercial Pro is licensed per seat at $99/seat/year (launch price, locked for life), with a 3-seat minimum. You choose the number of seats at Square checkout, and the quantity you select sets your licensed seat count. The regular price is $149/seat/year.

Which storage backends does DRK support?

Seven first-class backends — local disk, SMB/CIFS, SFTP, Amazon S3, Proxmox Backup Server, and Restic — plus 40+ additional clouds through Rclone. You bring your own storage on every plan.

Is encryption included on the Free plan?

Yes. AES-256 encryption at rest is included in every plan, including Free. Personal Pro and above add bring-your-own / customer-managed encryption keys.

What support do I get?

Support is best-effort and is not an SLA or guaranteed response time. The optional Priority Queue add-on ($750/year) prioritizes your requests, but it is still best-effort.

How do I activate my license after buying?

After checkout you’ll receive a license token by email. Paste it into the extension at Settings → About → “Have a license key?”, or set the DRK_LICENSE_KEY environment variable.
