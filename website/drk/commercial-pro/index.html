After you buy

  1. Choose your seat quantity and complete checkout on Square.
  2. Receive your license token by email.
  3. Open the extension: Settings → About → “Have a license key?”
  4. Paste the token (or set DRK_LICENSE_KEY). Your tier flips to Pro.
